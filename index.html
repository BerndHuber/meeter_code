Demo Meeter group meeting feedback

This is a demo of the Meeter group meeting analysis tool that classifies speech into information sharing, shared understanding, and other, as well as provides word counts. Make sure to use Google Chrome for this demo to work.

Click 'Start' when you start your meeting, 'Stop' when the meeting has ended, and 'Reset' to reset the demo.
